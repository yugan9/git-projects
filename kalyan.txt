Konidela Kalyan Babu,[4] better known by his stage name Pawan Kalyan, is 
an Indian film actor, producer, director, screenwriter, writer, and 
politician. His film works are predominantly in Telugu cinema. He is the 
younger brother of popular actor turned politician Chiranjeevi . Kalyan 
made his acting debut in the 1996 Telugu film Akkada Ammayi Ikkada 
Abbayi. In 1998, he starred in Tholi Prema, which won the National Film 
Award for Best Feature Film in Telugu for that year.
